About Me
Biography, Education & Memberships

Md. Emon Sardar is an early-career researcher and GIS specialist currently pursuing an MSc in Disaster Science and Emergency Management at Patuakhali Science and Technology University (PSTU), Bangladesh. With over two years of hands-on research experience, he specialises in geospatial analysis, disaster risk reduction, and climate resilience in coastal communities.

As a Graduate Research Assistant at the Bangladesh Network Office for Urban Safety (BNUS), BUET, he gained deep expertise in spatial data analysis, geoprocessing, and risk mapping using ArcGIS 10.8.2. His field work with ActionAid and PSTU brought him into direct engagement with 400+ coastal community respondents across Patuakhali — strengthening his evidence-based approach to climate adaptation research.

He is currently seeking fully-funded PhD or MSc opportunities at international institutions, with research interests spanning GIS, remote sensing, shoreline dynamics, coastal vulnerability, and community-based disaster risk management.
